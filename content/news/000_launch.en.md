---
title: "Launch of the Laboratory of Yanmar Data-Driven Sustainable Agriculture"
date: 2025-02-28
---

Kyoto University's Graduate School of Agriculture and Yanmar Agri Co., Ltd. established the university–industry joint course "Laboratory of Yanmar Data-Driven Sustainable Agriculture," effective February 1, 2025. Over the five years through January 2030, the partners will conduct joint research on data-driven sustainable agriculture.

The joint research aims to establish a data-driven farming system that, in addition to further advancing autonomous-driving and electrification technologies, collects data on fields, crop conditions, and soil analysis in the cloud to enable remote farm management. Alongside cooperation in lectures and research-project supervision within the Graduate School of Agriculture, cultivation and operation analyses involving students and research students will be carried out at Kyoto University's Kizu Farm.
