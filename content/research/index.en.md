# Research Themes

Toward establishing data-driven sustainable agriculture, the course pursues the themes below, advancing the research through field demonstrations at sites including Kizu Farm.

## Work systems with autonomous & electric machinery

Demonstrating integrated farming operation systems that use DX and GX technologies such as autonomous-driving and electric farm machinery.

## Electric machinery powered by renewable energy

Establishing integrated workflow systems and fixed-point observation techniques using electric machinery powered by renewable energy.

## Measurement & analysis of organic fertilizers (compost)

Measuring and analyzing the use of organic fertilizers (compost), including rice-husk biochar and bio-composters.

## Resource circulation via beneficial microorganisms

Conducting research and analysis on resource circulation, reduced material use, and improved productivity using beneficial microorganisms.

## Training the next generation

Developing the engineers and researchers who will lead the next generation of sustainable food production.
